How the forecast works

No machine-learning black box — just a clean idea executed well.

1

Listen 🛰️

Every 5 minutes, the poller records how many e-bikes sit at each of SF's 382 stations.

2

Learn 📈

Weeks of history become a baseline: what a typical Tuesday at 8:15am looks like, station by station.

3

Look 👀

When you open the map, it grabs the live count and measures how today differs from typical.

4

Glide ✨

That difference fades as the forecast looks further ahead — blending "right now" into "usually."

±0.5 bikes

Median forecast error, backtested against 400,000+ real readings. Nine out of ten predictions land within ±3.5 bikes.
